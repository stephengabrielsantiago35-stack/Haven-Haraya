Residence

A 2-bedroom retreat built for slow mornings and long evenings.

A 2-bedroom, 4-bed condo in City of Taguig with a full kitchen, open-plan living, balcony, and pool access — designed for guests who travel for stillness as much as for the city.

01

Sleeping Setup

The master bedroom features a queen bed with premium linens, while the second bedroom offers two single beds — ideal for families or groups of friends. A sofa bed in the living area accommodates two additional guests, bringing the total capacity to six.

02

Living & Dining

The open-plan living area is furnished with a comfortable sofa, flat-screen TV, and dining table for four. Large windows fill the space with natural light, creating a bright and inviting atmosphere for meals, work, or relaxation.

03

Kitchen & Bath

The fully equipped kitchen includes a stove, refrigerator, microwave, and essential cookware. The bathroom is fitted with a modern walk-in shower, vanity, and complimentary toiletries for your stay.

04

Private Balcony

Step out onto your private balcony to enjoy fresh air and garden views. The outdoor space includes seating for two, perfect for morning coffee or evening drinks after a day exploring the city.

05

Self Check-In

Enjoy a contact-free arrival with smart-lock access. Check-in is available from 3:00 PM onwards, and the dedicated parking slot ensures a smooth arrival for guests traveling by car.

06

Building Amenities

As a guest of Haven Haraya, you have access to the condominium's swimming pool, landscaped grounds, and elevator. The building also features 24-hour security for peace of mind throughout your stay.
