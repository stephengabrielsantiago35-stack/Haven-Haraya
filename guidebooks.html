Traveler Advice

Tips for a smooth stay.

What to Pack

Manila is warm year-round, so light, breathable clothing is recommended. Bring swimwear to enjoy the resort-style pool, comfortable walking shoes for city outings, and a small umbrella during rainy months.

Getting Around Manila

Ride-hailing apps like Grab and InDrive are the easiest way to get around. Motorcycle taxis (Angkas, Move It, JoyRide) are popular during traffic. A designated parking slot is included with your stay.

Don't Miss

Quiet highlights of the community.

Sky Lounge Views

The roofdeck Sky Lounge offers panoramic views of Makati, Laguna de Bay, and the surrounding skyline. A beautiful spot for sunrise or sunset — open 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Resort-Style Swimming Pool

Enjoy the community's resort-style swimming pool for relaxed afternoons and quiet downtime. Pool access is subject to building guidelines and availability.
